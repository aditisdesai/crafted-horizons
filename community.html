Community & Opportunity

Building something together—no hype, just real growth

The opportunity

I'm part of The Latitude Collective—a community of travel advisors who've built businesses around travel. Journalists, engineers, educators, parents, corporate professionals. People with full-time jobs who wanted something more aligned with how they actually want to live.

If you've ever thought about earning from travel—your own bookings, helping friends, growing a client base—this might be worth exploring. It's not for everyone. But if you're already traveling, already researching, already helping people plan trips... why not get paid for it?

I bring my decade of leadership experience to mentoring others in this space. The product thinking, the coaching, the systematic approach—it all translates.

What you get

When you join as an Independent Travel Advisor through The Latitude Collective, you gain:

  • Professional booking platforms — access to the same systems travel agents use
  • Agent-only rates — commission structures on bookings you make
  • Training & support — onboarding, continued learning, and community
  • Mentorship — guidance from advisors (like me) who've built this successfully
  • Flexibility — work around your schedule, build at your pace

This is a legitimate business model, not a side hustle fad. It requires learning, relationship-building, and consistent effort. But for the right person, it's a way to align income with lifestyle.

Who this is for

This model works best for people who:

  • Already love travel and help friends/family plan trips
  • Want more flexibility and control over their income
  • Are comfortable learning new systems and building relationships
  • Have a network of people who trust their recommendations
  • Want to build something that grows over time, not overnight

If that resonates, let's have a conversation. I'll share how I got started, what the learning curve looks like, and whether this aligns with what you're looking for.

My approach to mentoring

I spent a decade leading product and engineering teams—coaching people, breaking down complex problems, helping others build their skills systematically. That's how I approach mentoring in this space.

I'm not here to sell you on hype. I'm here to give you honest guidance about what works, what doesn't, and how to build this in a way that fits your life.

If you're curious, let's talk. No pressure, no sales pitch—just a real conversation about whether this opportunity makes sense for you.
