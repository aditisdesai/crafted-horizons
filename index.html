From building products to crafting journeys

For the past decade, I've led product and engineering teams—building digital experiences, coaching people, solving complex problems. But travel? That's always been how I really learn.

Not the checklist kind. The kind where you sit with locals over food you can't pronounce, where the TV version of a place doesn't match reality, where a random conversation shifts how you see the world.

I've explored 20+ destinations across continents, always researching obsessively, always asking "what's the story here?" Now I bring that same intentionality—the product thinking, the attention to detail—to designing travel experiences for others.

Crafted Horizons is what happens when engineering discipline meets wanderlust.

Lifestyle & Freedom

Building a life where travel isn't just a two-week break

What if travel wasn't an expense?

Here's what I learned: you can earn while you explore. Not through influencer hype or digital nomad clichés—through something more tangible.

I book travel for myself, family, friends. I earn commission on those bookings. I design trips for clients who value expertise. I also help others start their own travel businesses—people like you who love travel and want to align their income with their lifestyle.

This isn't passive income in the "get rich quick" sense. It's passive in the "your trips fund future trips" sense. It's freedom in the "you control your schedule" sense.

Travel becomes sustainable when it pays for itself.
