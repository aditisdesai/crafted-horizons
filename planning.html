Travel Planning

How I remove the stress and research burden from your next trip

How I help

  • Custom itineraries shaped by your interests (not a template)
  • Research-driven recommendations from someone who's actually been there
  • Family trips, milestone celebrations, group adventures
  • Cruises & all-inclusive options (with honest tradeoffs)
  • Support before, during, and after—like having a travel partner on call

As an Independent Travel Advisor, I have access to professional travel platforms, agent-only rates, and trusted partners that I've used to successfully plan trips for friends and family. Now I'm offering that same service more widely.

The process

  1. You share your vision — dates, budget, travel style, what matters to you
  2. I research & curate — options aligned to your preferences, not commission
  3. You book with confidence — and I stay with you through the journey

The more detail you share upfront, the faster I can tailor recommendations that actually fit.

Why work with me

I'm not selling you a package. I'm not working off commission incentives. I'm using the same research approach I use for my own travel—obsessive attention to detail, understanding the story behind a place, and designing an experience that fits how you actually want to travel.

My services cost you nothing extra. I earn through industry partnerships and agent rates, but my recommendations are driven by what makes sense for your trip—not what earns me more.

Think of it as having a travel partner who's done the homework, has professional access, and cares about getting it right.
