Lifestyle & Freedom

Building a life where travel isn't just a two-week break

What if travel wasn't an expense?

Here's what I learned: you can earn while you explore. Not through influencer hype or digital nomad clichés—through something more tangible.

I book travel for myself, family, friends. I earn commission on those bookings. I design trips for clients who value expertise. I also help others start their own travel businesses—people like you who love travel and want to align their income with their lifestyle.

This isn't passive income in the "get rich quick" sense. It's passive in the "your trips fund future trips" sense. It's freedom in the "you control your schedule" sense.

How it works

As an Independent Travel Advisor with InteleTravel and part of The Latitude Collective, I have access to professional booking systems, agent-only rates, and industry partnerships.

When I book travel—for myself, for clients, for friends—I earn commission. That commission helps fund my own travel and gives me the flexibility to build this business on my terms.

The same model is available to others who want to do the same. If you're already helping friends plan trips, already researching destinations obsessively, already thinking about travel constantly—why not structure that into something that pays?

Travel becomes sustainable when it pays for itself.

Who this is for

This model works for people who:

  • Already love planning their own travel (and helping others plan theirs)
  • Want more flexibility in how they earn income
  • Appreciate the idea of trips funding future trips
  • Have a network of people who trust their recommendations
  • Want to build something aligned with how they want to live

It's not a get-rich-quick scheme. It's a legitimate business model that requires effort, learning, and relationship-building. But if travel is already part of your life, it might be worth exploring.
